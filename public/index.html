Meema

Portable password security made easy.

Everyone relies on passwords these days - but it's far too easy to break them. If you store them in an online system, that system can itself be hacked to gain your passwords; meanwhile, local storage systems can be easily stolen or physically broken into.

Enter Meema.

If one system isn't secure, why not use two? It's local password storage and cloud password storage working together. And it's about time.

The cloud storage stores a piece of your password, but not enough to reproduce the whole thing.

The local storage also stores some of your password, but still not enough to get the whole thing.

Only with access to both systems can you - and only you - get access to your passwords.
That's the power of Meema.
